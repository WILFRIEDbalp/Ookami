Simone Mahler

Role Artistic direction, design, communication digial, packaging
COMPANY Simone Mahler

The simone mahler institute contacted us for a redesign of their pack and logo modification. The angle which was chosen for the artistic direction was to put back in the center the natural plants chosen in the beauty products.

The other point was a resentment between the technicality and the cells and then the morning dew which would be distinguished by a pattern between cell and dew.

On each pack is present all the plants that compose it as a floral composition. The floral composition is colored in a specific way, each color corresponds to a range of the brand for example pink for the face.

The prestige range is decorated with the plants present with gilding. We keep the same idea for office supplies and business cards.
